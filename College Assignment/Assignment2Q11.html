About Us

National Institute of Technology Jamshedpur, is an Institute of National Importance located at Jamshedpur, Jharkhand, India. Established as a Regional Institute of Technology on 15 August 1960, it was upgraded to National Institute of Technology on 27 December 2002 with the status of a Deemed University.National Institute of Technology (NIT) Jamshedpur is ranked at 130 th in the Engineering category by NIRF Ranking 2019. The College currently holds a total strength of 2885 including male and female students as well as a faculty of 153 highly qualified members.

We are leader in Education since 1960

National Institute of Technology, Jamshedpur takes extreme pride in the fact that it has been occupying a unique position in imparting technological education to Indian youth. Since its inception, the institute has had a vision to provide quality technical education and to facilitate scientific and technological research, coupled with a mission to develop human potential to its zenith.
